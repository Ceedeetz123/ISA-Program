About me


Personal Profile

I am a music lover and as a hobby I write earphone reviews for fun and to increase my earphone storage. I am not really a critical listener as I just listen to a bunch of music from different genres and I don’t look for details every time I listen to a music, I just note down anything that is obvious e.g. is the bass overpowering the vocals? and is the soundstage deep? I typically listen to music for hours every day when reviewing an earphone. I can determine a earphones overall sound by comparing other earphones and my overall listening experience of the earphone.
